Can I create a association between two posts (e.g. for multilingual posts)?

kleinfreund Thursday, January 01, 2015

I have a bilingual site. For each post, I like to link to its counterpart. Currently, there is no logical connection between the two posts.

The German posts (default) reside in /_posts while the English posts are located in /en/_posts. The category en which is automatically created by having the posts inside /en is used for permalinks.

My question is: Is there a way I can entangle two posts (German and English) and create the links to the respective counterpart without writing them directly in my posts? What would be the strategy for that?

(I let Pages do the build, so plugins won't work. Also, I don't want working code, just a small entry point.)

mattr- Friday, January 02, 2015

There's not really any way to do this currently. It's up to the site creator to determine how they want to handle multilingual sites and how they link their posts together.

troyswanson Sunday, January 04, 2015

As @mattr- said, there isn't anything built in, but you could create a loop in your posts layout which looks for the translated counterpart and outputs some HTML if it finds something.

Hope this helps.
