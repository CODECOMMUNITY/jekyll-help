Sorting yaml front matter variable with heterogeneous data

DonnieWest Tuesday, May 05, 2015

I am creating a simple song site with Jekyll and my songs are represented as posts with the following front matter:

---
layout: post
title: "My Song"
date: 2015-05-05T13:40:02-05:00
song: 3
---

I am porting over old paper bound books to this site, and unfortunately the songs are ordered by both number and letters (The song above could be 3 or it could be "A"). My attempts to sorting them are failing.

When doing this: {% assign posts = site.posts | sort:'song' %}

It throws this error: Error: comparison of Jekyll::Post with Jekyll::Post failed

Any way I can work around this?

budparr Tuesday, May 05, 2015

Try putting quote marks around the song value, like song: '3'
