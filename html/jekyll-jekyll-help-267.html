kyrsideris Thursday, April 02, 2015

I had exactly the same problem and site.url or site.baseurl inside _config.yml were looking sane. What I did to overcome the problem was, as @kleinfreund suggested, to prepend: site.url or/and site.baseurl whenever was missing. The main damage to your css comes from _includes/head.html which should be changed from:

<link rel="stylesheet" href="{{ "/css/main.css" | prepend: site.baseurl }}">

to:

<link rel="stylesheet" href="{{ "/css/main.css" | prepend: site.baseurl | prepend: site.url }}">

Hope that helps!
