mmistakes Wednesday, March 04, 2015

Not sure what Markdown converter you're using, but Kramdown can automatically generate a table of contents for you. It gives you control over the placement of the toc too, you just place a {:toc} tag where you'd like it to appear and Kramdown does the rest.

There are also several Javascript solutions that can do the same thing.
