re: xcode Just make sure it's installed. You may encounter gems or packages that you need to compile on your machine and xcode pre-installs GCC and what not for you.

re: homebrew They are not the only package manager, MacPorts and Fink are good alternatives.

re: code editor The latest hotness on the Mac scene is probably Sublime Text. I've been using it for 1.5 years now and I love it. Once you discover and start building your own snippets you'll be hooked.
