alfredxing Monday, August 04, 2014

This warning was introduced recently in jekyll/jekyll#2620. Officially, in YAML, layout: null is the proper way to specify no layout, but there's an issue open discussing usage of nil. For now, I'd recommend changing layout: nil at the the top of your sitemap.xml file to layout: null.
